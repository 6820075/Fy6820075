Abstract
  
   
    

      Many new methods of harvesting electrical energy have been discovered.
      Triboelectric nanogenerators could be the small, cheap solution to find 
      sustainable replacements to power sources that harm the worlds environment. 
      Triboelectric nanogenerators have the capability to be used in many areas, 
      for example in clothes in soles of shoes and on human joints. This article 
      explains the process of the creation of electric energy and how much energy 
      a Triboelectric nanogenerator can produce. It also explores, how human activity
      could easily play such a big role in creating green energy.
